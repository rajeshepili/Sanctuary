### Hi, I’m Rajesh! 👋

Thank you for taking the time to read this.

My motivation for creating this application comes from my need to build habits (I’ve been slacking on physical workouts 🏃‍♂️) and to journal my thoughts ✍️. I’ve read that consistent self-reflection can drive self-realization and personal growth 🌱, but I often struggled to stay consistent with my habits.

I wanted a tool that I could fully own and run locally 🖥️, rather than relying on someone else’s service or hosting my data on their servers. I also wanted something visually appealing, like beautiful landscapes 🌄 that greet you when you open the app, which I found missing in most habit-tracking apps.

This project helped me build and track habits effectively, while also giving me the opportunity to practice a feature-based codebase structure 🧠 (still learning, but progressing steadily).

This is also my "first" open source project 🥳 (I’ve tried releasing one in the past, but didn’t fully understand the scope and kind of vibe-coded 😅). I’m excited to see it grow into a community in the future.
